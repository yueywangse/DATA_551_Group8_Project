# Code of Conduct
## 1. Our Commitment

We are committed to creating a safe, inclusive, respectful, and harassment-free collaborative environment.

Regardless of participants':

- Gender or gender identity

- Sexual orientation

- Race or ethnic background

- Religious beliefs

- Nationality

- Academic background

- Technical experience

All should be treated equally.

We expect all participants in this project to communicate and collaborate professionally, rationally, and respectfully.

## 2. Encouraged Behaviors

We encourage the following behaviors:

- Using respectful and inclusive language

- Expressing different viewpoints rationally

- Accepting constructive feedback

- Focusing on the issue itself, not the individual

- Maintaining professionalism and restraint in discussions

We support open discussion but oppose emotional or aggressive expression.

## 3. Unacceptable Behaviors

The following behaviors will not be accepted:

- Discrimination or harassment in any form

- Personal attacks or insults

- Excluding, belittling, or mocking others

- Maliciously interrupting discussions

- Maliciously spreading misinformation

- Academic dishonesty

Such behaviors will undermine teamwork and the learning atmosphere.

## 4. Problem Reporting Mechanism

In case of inappropriate behavior, the relevant personnel may:

1. Resolve the issue privately if appropriate.

2. Explain the situation to the project team.

3. Report to the course instructor or teaching assistant if necessary.

All feedback will be taken seriously and kept confidential as much as possible.

## 5. Enforcement Methods

Personnel who violate this code of conduct may:

- Be reminded to adjust their behavior.

- Be restricted from participating in discussions.

- In serious cases, the matter will be referred to the course coordinator.

This code aims to ensure a positive, healthy, and efficient collaborative environment.
